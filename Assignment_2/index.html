Virtual reality (VR) art therapy is a type of therapy where people use a VR headset to create art in a virtual space. Instead of using paper or paints, you can draw or paint in 3D, like in a video game world. It helps people express their feelings and work through problems in a safe, creative way. This kind of therapy can be really helpful for people who have trouble talking about their emotions. VR makes it easier to relax and focus on the art, which can improve mental health.
